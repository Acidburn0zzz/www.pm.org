Perl Mongers

Perl Mongers is a loose association of 255 international Perl User Groups.

  1. What are Perl Mongers?

    Individual groups vary in what they actually do, in most cases Perl users in a particular location get together periodically to discuss Perl and related topics, or just to socialize.

    There are currently 255 Perl monger groups around the world
