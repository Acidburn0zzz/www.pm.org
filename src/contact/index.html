Please use the email addresses below to contact Perl Mongers

support <at> pm.org

The address for local Perl Monger group leaders to request things from from the sysadmins - i.e. changes to leadership or other details in the list of groups.

webmaster <at> pm.org

Any comments or suggestions about the web site.

Emails sent to either of those addresses go to an RT queue. Usually you will receive a reply from RT within a few minutes. SMTP congestion may occasionally cause delays. If you haven't received an e-mail reply 24 hours later feel free to contact jhannah on irc.perl.org.
